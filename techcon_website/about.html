About TechCon 2024

TechCon 2024 is the premier technology conference in Africa, bringing together innovators, developers, and tech enthusiasts from across the continent.

Join us for a day of inspiring talks, hands-on workshops, and networking opportunities with industry leaders.

History

TechCon has been a staple in the African tech community since its inception in 2010. Each year, we strive to bring the latest trends and technologies to our attendees.

From humble beginnings, TechCon has grown into a must-attend event for anyone passionate about technology.

Mission

Our mission is to foster innovation and collaboration within the African tech ecosystem. We aim to provide a platform for knowledge sharing and networking that empowers individuals and organizations.

We believe that by connecting people and ideas, we can drive the future of technology in Africa.

Past Speakers

Over the years, TechCon has hosted a diverse lineup of speakers, including industry leaders, tech entrepreneurs, and visionary thinkers.

Our past speakers have shared their insights on a wide range of topics, from artificial intelligence to blockchain technology.
